GDPR Compliance

SmartSpend is designed with privacy at its core. Here's how we comply with the General Data Protection Regulation for our users in the European Union and European Economic Area.

📅 Last updated: April 1, 2026
🇪🇺 GDPR Article 13/14 Compliant
🔒 Data Minimization Principle

🇪🇺 For EU/EEA Users: You have specific rights under GDPR. SmartSpend is committed to honoring all of them. Your financial data is processed only with your explicit consent, and you can withdraw it at any time.

01
Data Controller

SmartSpend Technologies Pvt. Ltd. acts as the Data Controller for all personal data collected through the SmartSpend platform. Our Data Protection Officer can be contacted at dpo@smartspend.in.

As we are based in India, cross-border transfers of EU personal data to India are conducted under Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) as approved by the European Commission.

02
Legal Basis for Processing

Under GDPR Article 6, we process your personal data on the following legal bases:

  • Contract performance — Processing necessary to deliver the SmartSpend service you subscribed to
  • Legitimate interests — Security monitoring, fraud prevention, and service improvement (we always balance this against your rights)
  • Consent — Marketing communications and optional analytics. You can withdraw consent at any time.
  • Legal obligation — Compliance with applicable Indian and EU law

We do not rely on "legitimate interests" to override your fundamental rights or process your financial data for any purpose beyond providing the core service.

03
Your Rights Under GDPR

👁️
Right to Access
Request a copy of all personal data we hold about you (Art. 15)
✏️
Right to Rectification
Correct inaccurate or incomplete personal data (Art. 16)
🗑️
Right to Erasure
Request deletion of your personal data ("Right to be Forgotten") (Art. 17)
⏸️
Right to Restrict
Restrict processing of your data in specific circumstances (Art. 18)
📦
Right to Portability
Receive your data in a machine-readable format (Art. 20)
🚫
Right to Object
Object to processing based on legitimate interests (Art. 21)

To exercise any of these rights, email dpo@smartspend.in with the subject line "GDPR Rights Request". We will respond within 30 days. Requests are free of charge.

04
Data Transfers Outside the EU

Your data is stored on AWS servers in India (Mumbai region). As India is not currently recognized as having "adequate protection" by the European Commission, we use Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) as the transfer mechanism for any EU personal data.

Our sub-processors (Razorpay, AWS, Anthropic, SendGrid, Sentry) are all bound by Data Processing Agreements that include GDPR-compliant data transfer mechanisms. A list of current sub-processors is available upon request.

05
Data Breach Notification

In the event of a personal data breach, SmartSpend will notify the relevant supervisory authority within 72 hours of becoming aware of the breach, where feasible. We will also notify affected users without undue delay if the breach is likely to result in a high risk to your rights and freedoms.

06
Supervisory Authority

If you are located in the EU/EEA and believe we have not handled your data in accordance with GDPR, you have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection supervisory authority. A list of EU supervisory authorities is available at edpb.europa.eu.

We would, however, appreciate the opportunity to address your concerns first. Please contact us at dpo@smartspend.in before escalating to a supervisory authority.

07
Privacy by Design

GDPR's "Privacy by Design" principle is embedded in how we build SmartSpend:

  • We collect only the minimum data necessary for each feature
  • AI processing uses anonymized data where possible
  • All new features undergo a Data Protection Impact Assessment (DPIA)
  • Employee access to personal data is strictly role-based and logged
  • Data retention periods are minimal and automatically enforced
