Short Description (25 words)
SmartSpend is an AI-powered personal finance app that helps Indians track expenses, set budgets, and build better money habits — beautifully and effortlessly.
📋 Copy Text
Full Company Description (75 words)
SmartSpend Technologies Pvt. Ltd. is a Bengaluru-based fintech startup founded in 2023, building India's most intelligent personal finance platform. The app uses AI, OCR receipt scanning, and voice entry to help users track spending, understand patterns, and make smarter financial decisions. With over 24,000 active users and ₹840 crore tracked monthly, SmartSpend has earned a 4.9-star rating on the App Store and was recognized as India's #1 Finance App on Product Hunt 2025.
📋 Copy Text
Founder Bio — Aryan Khanna, CEO
Aryan Khanna is the Co-founder and CEO of SmartSpend. A Razorpay alumnus with 8 years in Indian fintech, Aryan co-founded SmartSpend in 2023 after experiencing firsthand how fragmented and frustrating personal finance management was for everyday Indians. He is based in Bengaluru.
📋 Copy Text
Founder Bio — Shreya Rao, CTO
Shreya Rao is the Co-founder and CTO of SmartSpend. An IIT Delhi computer science graduate and former machine learning engineer at Flipkart, Shreya leads all engineering and AI development at SmartSpend. She is passionate about building performant, privacy-first financial products at scale.
