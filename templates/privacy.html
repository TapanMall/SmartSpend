Privacy Policy

We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains exactly what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights as a SmartSpend user.

📍 Bengaluru, India
✅ GDPR Compliant
🇮🇳 DPDP Act 2023 Compliant

🛡️ TL;DR: Your financial data belongs to you. We never sell it, never share it with advertisers, and you can delete everything at any time. SmartSpend is a tool to help you — not a data broker.

01
Information We Collect

Account Information

When you create a SmartSpend account, we collect your name, email address, and a hashed password. If you sign in with Google, we receive only your name and email from Google's OAuth service — nothing more.

Financial Data You Enter

SmartSpend stores the expense and income data you voluntarily add. This includes transaction amounts, categories, dates, merchant names, notes, and any receipt images you upload for OCR scanning.

  • Transaction records (amount, category, merchant, date, notes)
  • Budget goals and limits you set
  • Receipt images (processed and then deleted within 24 hours)
  • Voice entries (transcribed and deleted immediately after processing)

Technical Data

We automatically collect standard technical data: IP address, browser type, device type, and app usage patterns. This helps us diagnose bugs and improve performance.

02
How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to provide and improve the SmartSpend service. Specifically:

  • To power the expense tracking, budgeting, and analytics features
  • To generate AI-powered insights and spending recommendations
  • To send you product notifications, alerts, and (with your consent) email updates
  • To authenticate your identity and protect your account
  • To diagnose bugs and improve app performance
  • To respond to your support requests

🚫 We will never use your financial data to serve you targeted advertising, build behavioral profiles for sale, or share it with insurance companies, lenders, or any financial institution without your explicit, written consent.

03
Data Storage & Security

All SmartSpend data is stored on servers in India, in compliance with Indian data localization requirements. We use industry-standard security measures including:

  • AES-256 encryption for all data at rest
  • TLS 1.3 for all data in transit
  • JWT token-based authentication with short expiry windows
  • Zero plain-text storage of passwords (bcrypt hashing)
  • Regular third-party security audits
  • SOC 2 Type II certification (in progress, target Q3 2026)

In the event of a data breach that affects your personal data, we will notify you by email within 72 hours of discovery, as required by law.

04
Sharing & Third Parties

We work with a small number of trusted third-party service providers to operate SmartSpend. These providers are bound by strict data processing agreements and may only use your data to provide their specific service to us:

  • Razorpay — Payment processing (they never see your expense data)
  • Amazon Web Services (AWS India) — Cloud infrastructure and storage
  • Anthropic Claude API — AI insights generation (anonymized data only)
  • SendGrid — Transactional email delivery
  • Sentry — Error monitoring (no financial data included)

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party. We may disclose data if required by Indian law or a valid court order, in which case we will notify you unless prohibited from doing so by law.

05
Cookies & Tracking

SmartSpend uses minimal, essential cookies required for the app to function (authentication tokens, preferences). We use a single analytics service (self-hosted Plausible Analytics) that is privacy-friendly, collects no personal identifiers, and is not shared with any third party.

We do not use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or any advertising network tracking. For full details, see our Cookie Policy.

06
Your Rights

Under the Digital Personal Data Protection Act 2023 (DPDP Act) and GDPR (for EU users), you have the following rights:

  • Access — Request a full export of all data we hold about you
  • Correction — Request that we correct inaccurate personal data
  • Deletion — Request permanent deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Portability — Receive your data in a machine-readable format (CSV/JSON)
  • Withdraw Consent — Opt out of non-essential communications at any time
  • Complaint — Lodge a complaint with the Data Protection Board of India

To exercise any of these rights, email us at privacy@smartspend.in or use the "Delete My Data" option in your account settings. We will respond within 30 days.

07
Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, all personal data is permanently purged within 30 days. Anonymized, aggregated data (with no personal identifiers) may be retained for product analytics.

08
Children's Privacy

SmartSpend is intended for users aged 18 and above. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately at privacy@smartspend.in.

09
Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this Privacy Policy, we will notify you by email at least 14 days before the changes take effect. We will also update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of SmartSpend after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
