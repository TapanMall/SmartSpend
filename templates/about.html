Our Mission

Financial clarity shouldn't be a privilege.

We started SmartSpend in 2023 with one belief: every person, regardless of their financial background, deserves to understand where their money goes and how to make it work harder.

From a tiny flat in Bengaluru, four founders who were tired of messy spreadsheets and opaque banking apps decided to build something better — an intelligent, beautiful, and truly personal finance companion.

Today, SmartSpend serves over 24,000 users across India, helping them track, analyze, and improve their financial lives every single day.

Journey

From an idea to 24,000 users

Four friends in a Bengaluru apartment, fed up with messy Excel sheets, decide to build the expense tracker they always wished existed.
