Cookie Policy

We keep cookies to a minimum. Here's exactly what we set, why, and how you can control them.

📅 Last updated: April 1, 2026
🚫 No Ad Tracking
🔒 Privacy-First

🍪 Short version: We use only essential cookies for the app to work, plus one privacy-friendly analytics tool that uses no personal identifiers. No advertising cookies. Ever.

01
What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by your browser when you visit a website or use a web application. They help maintain your session, remember your preferences, and help us understand how our service is used.

02
Cookies We Use

Cookie Name Purpose Type Expiry
ss_auth_token Keeps you logged in securely via JWT authentication Essential 7 days
ss_refresh_token Refreshes your session without re-login Essential 30 days
ss_theme Remembers your dark/light mode preference Preference 1 year
ss_csrf Prevents cross-site request forgery attacks Essential / Security Session
plausible_ignore Opt-out from Plausible Analytics (set only if you opt out) Analytics (opt-out) 1 year

We use Plausible Analytics — a privacy-first analytics tool that is self-hosted in India, collects no personal identifiers, uses no cookies by default, and is not shared with any third party. It is fully compliant with GDPR and the DPDP Act.

03
Cookies We Do NOT Use

  • Google Analytics or any Google advertising products
  • Facebook Pixel or Meta advertising tools
  • Any third-party advertising or behavioral tracking cookies
  • Cross-site tracking technologies of any kind

04
Your Cookie Preferences

You can control your cookie preferences below. Note that disabling essential cookies will break core app functionality.

Essential Cookies
Required for authentication, security, and basic app function.
Preference Cookies
Remembers your theme, language, and UI preferences.
Analytics (Plausible)
Privacy-friendly, anonymous usage data to help us improve.
Advertising & Tracking
We don't use these. This toggle does nothing.

05
Browser Controls

You can also control cookies through your browser settings. Note that blocking essential cookies will prevent you from logging in. Most browsers allow you to view, delete, or block cookies under Settings → Privacy.

For more information on managing cookies, visit allaboutcookies.org.
